Intro

Passionate about working in Machine Learning and Deep Learning, Generative AI.Experienced in Python, Java, C++, Algorithms, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning with a solid theoretical understanding. I have completed my M. Tech in Data Science from the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) with a CGPA of 9.3. I have authored 10 research papers published in conferences such as WACV, ICASSP, CVIP, and ICSC. Additionally, I actively participate as a reviewer for conferences like EMNLP and ICASSP. I hold a portfolio of 10 patents, including 7 A1 grade patents. I am eager to bring my passion for innovation and research to the team.

Work

Lead Engineer | MAR 2021 - PRESENT

Senior Software Engineer | FEB 2019 - MAR 2021

Specifically targeting the Indian market, I conceptualized and implemented a multilingual texting feature to cater to the linguistic diversity prevalent in the region. Additionally, I designed and deployed a robust on-device grammatical error correction system, contributing to the seamless and improved quality of text communication. My efforts significantly elevated the usability of mobile applications, addressing specific linguistic needs and fostering a more inclusive user experience.

Research Intern | JAN 2017 - JUL 2017
